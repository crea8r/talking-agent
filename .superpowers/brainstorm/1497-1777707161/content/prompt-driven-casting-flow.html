Prompt-Driven CosyVoice Casting Lab

No hard-coded characters. You describe a character, enter the test line, tune CosyVoice inputs, generate, compare, and optionally promote a take into a reusable zero-shot reference.

Main Workspace
1. Character Prompt
Describe the voice you want to cast.

Example:
young female voice, bright, playful, nimble, fairy-like, quick reactions, not squeaky, warm but mischievous
2. Session Text
The exact line to synthesize for this audition.

Example:
I found it. I knew there was still magic left in this room.
