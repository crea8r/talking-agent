# Repository Guidance

- This repository is a monorepo.
- Put runnable apps and discovery spikes in `apps/`.
- Put shared contracts, schemas, and utilities in `packages/`.
- Put durable product and architecture decisions in `docs/`.
- Prefer self-hosted and open-source solutions before paid managed services when both are viable.
- Keep spike apps small and disposable. Each app should validate one major unknown.
- Do not couple early spike apps to the final stack before the unknown they test has been proven.
